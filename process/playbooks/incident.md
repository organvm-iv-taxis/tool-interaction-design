# Incident Playbook

## Purpose
Restore service quickly while preserving learning.

## Entry Criteria
- Live issue impacts reliability, correctness, or delivery.

## Procedure
1. Assign incident commander and owner.
2. Triage severity and isolate blast radius.
3. Execute rollback or hotfix strategy.
4. Communicate status and ETA.
5. Capture timeline, root cause, and corrective actions.

## Exit Criteria
- Service stability restored.
- Post-incident actions are assigned with owners.

## Outputs
- Incident summary + remediation backlog items.
