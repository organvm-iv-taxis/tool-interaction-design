# Monetization Lanes

## Services Lane
Offer implementation and governance hardening engagements.
- KPI: active opportunities, conversion rate, average contract size.

## Products Lane
Ship reusable process kits (templates, checklists, policy packs).
- KPI: assets shipped, download/sales count, customer activation.

## Advisory Lane
Provide operating-system coaching and scorecard reviews.
- KPI: sessions/month, retention, net promoter feedback.

## Weekly Operating Rule
Each sprint must advance all three lanes by at least one measurable step.
